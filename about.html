About Jay's Jewelries

Born in Labone, Worn Worldwide

Jay's Jewelries began with a simple vision: to create jewelry that celebrates African elegance while meeting the standards of the world's finest luxury houses. Founded by Jacob Nii Adjetey Sowah in the heart of Labone, Accra, our atelier has grown from a small workshop into a beloved brand serving customers across Ghana, West Africa, Europe, and North America.

From our roots in Labone, every piece tells a story — of heritage, craftsmanship, and the confident modern wearer. We source ethically, work with master artisans, and believe luxury should be accessible without compromising quality.
